About Us

Our Corporate Mission and Vision is to provide a superior technology and be the mold fabrication corporation that will light and guide the other mold maker and injection companies in the Philippines.
Our company is committed to provide high quality fabricated molds in a timely, competitive cost and effective manner that will ensure customer’s satisfaction. We are also committed to provide appropriate training and education by enhancing and combining Filipino, Chinese, Japanese and Taiwanese knowledge that promotes new ideas and high technology.
Lastly, we are committed to contribute on employees better and quality of living. We take in satisfying the needs of our customer. Our technical staff assures that we provide a good design and easy to maintain mold and also provide time table bar chart for easy mold development monitoring. For the convenience and satisfaction of our customer, we assure a good after service policy.

JULY 8, 2008

Polaris Mold Fabrication Corporation was Established and Located at Carmona Cavite.
On October 2008 Started The Company Commercial Operation.

In The Year 2009

Partnership started with Sister of Mary School for OJT arrangement and manpower source.

In the Year 2010

Polaris was certified in ISO 9000 and ISO 14000 at Carmona Cavite.

In The Year 2019

The Factory was transferred from Carmona Cavite to FPIP Sto. Tomas Batangas.

In the Year 2021

Polaris has obtained PEZA's approval of Plastic Injection Molding Business.

Quality and Environmental Policy

For us to achieve our goal of fabricating and delivering good quality and environmental friendly molds, we are extending the following quality policy to be followed and implemented within the whole organization:
  • The company shall promote new ideas and technology by applying Taiwanese knowledge combine with Filipino resources in molding by giving continuous training and education.
  • Employees shall keep the machines and equipments in good condition at all times for the efficient and safe operation in the workplace.
  • The company shall follow and comply with all the quality and environmental standards set.
  • Our company is committed to take an active role in the world wide campaign for safe and healthy environment.
  • Applying the concept of Continuous Improvement.
